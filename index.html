How This Becomes a Virtual Power Plant

We aggregate many small, tree-like DER nodes (PV + batteries + V2G-capable EVs) and let a market-based controller decide who buys/sells power every few minutes. This is the same principle used in Sekou Crawford’s NASA work on “Power Management in Regenerative Life Support Systems Using Market-Based Control.”

As we grow, we match the NREL “DER Aggregation” business model to sell flexibility and services in deregulated markets — without needing a legacy utility to own everything.
