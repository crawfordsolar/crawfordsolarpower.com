EDEN: Electrified Distributed Energy Network

The Electrified Distributed Energy Network (EDEN) connects solar tree groves into a coordinated system of local generation and storage. Each grove helps stabilize the community grid while powering homes, churches, and electric vehicle charging hubs.

Solar Trees — What Are They?!

Solar trees are sculptural, space-efficient energy systems that mimic nature’s design — collecting sunlight through photovoltaic leaves and storing it in integrated batteries. They beautify communities while producing clean power locally.
